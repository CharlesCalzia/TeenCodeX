FAQs

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where people come together to create practical solutions to real-world problems using software or hardware. These events can be in person or virtual, and can be a great opportunity to connect with other people and build a cool project!

Do I need to find a team?

No, teams will be randomly allocated on the day and will be up to 5 people.

How do I register?

You must register for the hackathon by filling out this form:

What are the judging criteria?

  • Design and implementation
  • Live pitch
  • Social impact: how much does the project solve a problem
  • Technical complexity: how difficult is the project to develop and how technically advanced is it
